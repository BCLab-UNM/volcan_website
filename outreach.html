Outreach

Albuquerque, New Mexico

Bandelier Elementary School

Matthew Fricke and Carter Frost visited one of the 5th Grade classes at Bandelier Elementary School. We discussed the hurtles of observing volcanoes as well as forging strategies with the students. The students had the opportunity to operate the Swarmie Robots using only the onboard sensors and controllers to locate a resource, pick it up and deliver it to a collection zone.
